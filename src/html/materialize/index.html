Who are we?

We are a group of dedicated, passionate, and committed indiviuals from the American International School of Utah who have teamed up to compete in the 2016 First Robotics Competition.

What is First Robotics?

First Robotics is an international competition in which high school teams spend six weeks building a game playing robot to compete against other team's robots.
